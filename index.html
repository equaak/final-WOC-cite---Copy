Sarzhan

Hello everyone! We are Atlas. We are a community of teenagers who strive for their dreams, want to make the world a better place and break down boundaries. Atlas is a place where teens learn to be proactive, open-minded and independent. In our NFT collection, we displayed the values of a teenager of the 21st century. The collection consists of 365 pictures that depict the path of a teenager in exploring and discovering himself and the world around him for a year. This path is not ideal: there are difficulties with family, friends, internal conflicts. But in the end, all this makes us antifragile. This is a collection about the future generation. NFT Atlas.
